# INC-2025-014 — Checkout down after inventory URL change

## Summary
Checkout returned 500 for 4 minutes after a deploy. Error rate peaked at 0.39.

## Root cause
A configuration commit pointed `INVENTORY_SERVICE_URL` to a port with no
listener (`:9999`). Every checkout call hung on the stock reservation request
and returned 500. Symptoms and remediation are identical to a standard config
regression.

## Resolution
Reverted the offending commit; CI redeployed the previous configuration.
error_rate returned below 0.01 within 80 seconds. Followed RB-01.

## Duration
4m 05s. SLA impact ≈ €610 at €150/min.
