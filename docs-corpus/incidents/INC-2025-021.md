# INC-2025-021 — Product and pay routes 500 after field rename

## Summary
`/product` and `/pay` returned 500 for 6 minutes. Catalogue read and checkout
totals both failed.

## Root cause
A commit renamed the `price_eur` field to `priceEUR` in `products.json` without
updating the code that reads it. Every access raised `KeyError: 'price_eur'`.
A data schema mismatch (see RB-02).

## Resolution
Reverted the rename commit to restore the data/code pair atomically. Routes
returned 200 immediately after redeploy. Followed RB-02.

## Duration
6m 12s. SLA impact ≈ €930 at €150/min.
